About Me

I grew passionate of art in general during my college days back in the 90s. I used to do various pencil sketches, brush painting, acrylic painting etc. During my masters course, I enrolled in arts course, where I was taught painting and art to an advanced level over a semester. Whenever I had time at home, I used to paint and then after having been a professor for 20+ years, I resumed my hobby again in 2021 during the lockdown, and over time have painted 15-20 canvases! Having this passion in me, I would love to share this cretivity and knowledge to those interested!
